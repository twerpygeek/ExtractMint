What it extracts

Dates, transaction descriptions, references, withdrawals, deposits, tax fields, and running balances where the statement exposes them.

Why balance columns matter

The running balance gives accountants and operators a quick way to spot missing rows or misread debit and credit values.

Export formats

Download Excel XLSX, CSV, Google Docs-ready DOCX, and PDF summaries from the same extracted data.

Best fit

  • Text-based PDF bank statements with transaction tables.
  • Bookkeepers converting client statements into spreadsheets.
  • Founders or operators preparing monthly reconciliation files.
  • Teams that want a browser-side workflow before adding a backend queue.
Can ExtractMint convert any bank statement?

It works best with text-based statement PDFs. Scanned statements can work through OCR, but difficult scans should be reviewed before export.

Does the file leave my computer?

The current static app processes files in the browser. A future SaaS backend should add explicit retention controls and audit logs.
